Bookmark Sites Open Project

Idea:

All most every websites nowadays stores cookies on your computer's local store, to give users a better experience on the site. I thought about the same idea, but instead of storing cookies on your computer's local store, I store your favorites websites.

Description:

Bookmark sites is a single landing webpage that allow the using go enter their websites url and name to save it for future easy access. The bookmark site was build on February 18th, 2018.

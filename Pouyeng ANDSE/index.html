Burkina Faso

Burkina Faso sits at the edge of the Shael. About twice the size of Colorado, it is one of the smallest territories in West Africa with a sqaure kilometers of 270,200 which ranked them the 75th largest country in the world by area. But is is one of the most heavily populated. It has a population of 18,365,123 and it is the 59th largest country in the world by population.

Pouytenga

Pouytenga is the capital and largest city in the Department of Kouritenga Province in Burkina Faso. It has a population of about 102,000 (2016 estimate).
